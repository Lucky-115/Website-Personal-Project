Testing your knowledge of Kana characters

On this page, you can test your knowledge of Kana characters you've already learnt. The test is customizable to enhance Your experience. By clicking on the Gear button, you can open the customizability menu, where you can select from a variety of options. You can switch between Hiragana and Katakana by clicking on the corresponding buttons, as well as switching between Kana and the Latin Alphabet to display questions in. By clicking on the "Select Rows" Checkbox, you can choose which rows of characters exactly to practice. The test gives you four answer options. If you answer correcly, a green circle will appear on the screen, and a wrong answer will be marked by a red "X". Have fun testing your skills!
