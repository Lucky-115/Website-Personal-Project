What is Kana Learner!

Kana Learner is a website designed to let You learn Hiragana and Katakana characters from the japanese language. Here You can also test your knowledge and learn more about the Kana writing system!

Why should You learn Kana?

Learning Kana is a great introduction to Japanese and might spark your interest in learning the language as a whole. If you already want to learn Japanese, this is a good place to start, as being able to write and read Kana will be essential on your learning journey. It's also great for your mind, as it trains your memory skills and if you're not interested in Japanese, it can be a fun piece of trivia to know, along with its history and usage.

Where to start?

Here on Kana Learner, You can start by heading over to the "Learn Characters" page and receive further explanation there. You can also visit the "History of Kana" page to glean some information about the origin of Kana before diving in. No matter how you decide to start, take it slow and have fun!

Good luck with learning!
