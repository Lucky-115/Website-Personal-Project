Learning Kana with Kana Master

On this page, you are able to learn Kana by interacting with the two tables below. The character you move over with your cursor will appear over the tables with its reading next to it. You can memorize characters however You want, however I'd advise learning row by row (starting with "a, i, u, e, o", then "ka, ki, ku, ke, ko" and so on.) You can also write the characters down on your own or say them whenever you read them, the possibilities are endless. When you think you've memorized a row, you can go over to the "Test your knowledge" page to do a quiz and check how well you remember the characters. The test is customizable, and as such you can choose exactly which rows you'd like to test yourself on. Have fun!
